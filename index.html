SARASWATI PUJA

At CIMAGE College

THE COUNTDOWN HAS STARTED

For Saraswati Puja

Devotion And Worship

The Saraswati Puja celebration at CIMAGE begins with the invocation of the goddess Saraswati through prayers and rituals. All three branches of CIMAGE (Boring Road Branch, Vivekananda Marg Branch & Patliputra Branch) come alive with colourful decorations, adorned with flowers and rangolis, creating an atmosphere of reverence and joy. Students, dressed in traditional attire gathered at their respective branches for the worship of Maa Saraswati. They took the blessings of Maa Saraswati for knowledge and success in their academic pursuit.
